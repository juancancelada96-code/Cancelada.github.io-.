CHROMIUM: Exploring the Chemical Element -

This project focuses on the study of chromium (Cr), a transition metal with wide-ranging industrial, medical, and environmental applications. It will analyze its properties, uses, risks, and relevance in both everyday life and modern science. Chromium is a metallic chemical element belonging to Group 6 of the periodic table. It is represented by the symbol Cr and has an atomic number of 24.
